Spicy Sweetcorn Cakes

You will need:
  • 3 leeks
  • 5 green peppers
  • 2oz of sweetcorn
  • 2 pears
  • 390g of liver
Instructions:
  1. Score the leeks
  2. Wash the green peppers
  3. Weigh the sweetcorn
  4. Wash the pears
  5. Cut the liver into ribbons
  6. Put the liver in the saucepan
  7. Soak the sweetcorn
  8. Roll out the pears
  9. Slice the liver
  10. Score the green peppers
