Vancouver

  • Vancouver is a major city in western Canada

  • It is located in the Lower Mainland region of British Columbia.

  • Vancouver has the highest population density in Canada

  • Vancouver is one of the most ethnically and linguistically diverse cities in Canada.

  • Vancouver is consistently named as one of the top five worldwide cities for livability and quality of life.

  • In 2011, the city planned to become the greenest city in the world by 2020.

  • The city takes its name from George Vancouver, who explored the inner harbour of Burrard Inlet in 1792 and gave various places British names.

  • Vancouverism is the city's urban planning design philosophy.

  • Vancouver is one of Canada's warmest cities in the winter

  • Vancouver is also one of the wettest Canadian cities.
